Quick Answer

Yes — you can pay Progressive without logging in. Use the guest payment option on Progressive.com (no password, no account creation), or call 1(888)620-0950 to pay by phone with a representative. Both methods post your payment within minutes.

What You Need for Guest Payment

Three pieces of information verify your identity — nothing more. No password, no email, no account setup.

1

Policy Number

Found on your billing statement or insurance card.

2

ZIP Code

The 5-digit ZIP linked to your policy address.

3

Last Name

Must match the spelling on your policy exactly.

How to Pay Progressive Without Logging In

Six steps, under two minutes, fully encrypted.

1
Go to Progressive.com and select "Pay Without Logging In" or call 1(888)620-0950 directly.
2
Enter your policy number exactly as shown on your bill.
3
Enter your ZIP code and last name to verify your identity.
4
Choose your payment method — credit card, debit card, or bank account.
5
Select the amount to pay (full or partial) and review the details.
6
Click Confirm Payment and save your confirmation number.
Guest payments post immediately — usually within minutes. Need help mid-process? Call 1(888)620-0950 for live phone assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay my Progressive bill without logging in?

Yes. Progressive's guest payment option lets you pay without an account. Enter your policy number, ZIP code, and last name to verify, then pay by card or bank account in under 2 minutes.

What information do I need for guest payment?

You need your policy number, 5-digit ZIP code, and last name exactly as it appears on your policy. No password or account creation required.

Is guest payment safe without a login?

Yes. Guest payment uses the same SSL encryption and PCI-compliant processing as logged-in payments. Your card and bank details are protected end-to-end.

How fast does a no-login payment post?

Guest payments post immediately, within minutes of submission — the same speed as logged-in online payments.

What if I forgot my Progressive password?

Skip the reset entirely. Use guest payment to pay instantly with just your policy number, ZIP, and last name, or call 1(888)620-0950 for phone assistance.
