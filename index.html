Payment Method 6: Mail Payment by Check

What is mail payment? Traditional check payment sent by mail. Slowest method but still accepted. Takes 5-10 days to post. Good for those without online access.

How to pay Progressive by mail with check:

  1. Locate your Progressive bill (paper bill or print PDF)
  2. Find the mailing address printed on the back or bottom of bill
  3. Write a check in the full amount due
  4. Write the check number in your checkbook
  5. Make check payable to "Progressive" or specific payee listed
  6. Do NOT postdate the check
  7. Look at the back of your bill for specific mailing address
  8. Write the address on your envelope
  9. Include your policy number on the check memo line
  10. Put check and bill stub in envelope (if bill is multi-part)
  11. Seal the envelope
  12. Add postage stamp (check current postal rates)
  13. Drop mail in postal mailbox or post office
  14. Wait 5-10 business days for delivery and processing
  15. Payment posts to your account after Progressive receives check

✓ Important: Mail payments are recorded by postmark date, not delivery date. Keep copy of check for your records. This is the slowest payment method.

Frequently Asked Questions About Progressive Bill Pay

Find quick answers to the most common Progressive payment questions:

Can I pay my Progressive bill online without an account or login?
Yes! Use Progressive's "Pay Without Logging In" feature on their homepage. Enter your policy number, ZIP code, and last name. You can make a secure payment in under 2 minutes without creating an account or logging in. Alternatively, call 1(888)620-0950 for phone payment assistance as a guest.
What's the difference between one-time payment and AutoPay?
One-Time Payment: A single payment transaction. You pay once and are not charged again unless you submit another payment manually. Full control over timing and amount.

AutoPay: Automatic recurring payments on a set schedule (usually monthly). Once enabled, Progressive automatically charges your payment method on your billing date each month. You must manually cancel to stop AutoPay.
Does Progressive charge fees for online bill payment?
No. Progressive does not charge fees for online, app, phone, or bank (ACH) payments. Guest payments are also free. Mail payments are free. Late payments may incur late fees depending on your policy terms and state. Check your bill for specific fee details or call 1(888)620-0950.
How long does it take for my Progressive payment to post?
Online, app, phone, or guest payments: Post immediately (within minutes)
Bank transfer (ACH): 1-3 business days
Mail (check): 5-10+ business days from postmark

Always save your confirmation number. If a payment hasn't posted within the timeframe, call 1(888)620-0950 with your confirmation number.
What is Progressive's grace period for late payments?
Most Progressive policies have a grace period of 10-15 days after your due date. During this time, your policy remains active even if your payment is late. However, your policy can be canceled after the grace period ends. Call 1(888)620-0950 before your due date if you need to discuss payment options or possible extensions.
Can I make multiple payments in one billing period?
Yes! You can make as many one-time payments as you need during your billing period. This is helpful if you want to pay your bill in installments or make a partial payment now and a full payment later. Each payment is processed separately and posts individually.
What payment methods does Progressive accept?
Progressive accepts: credit cards, debit cards, bank accounts (ACH from checking or savings), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, checks by mail, money orders, and eChecks. Choose the method that works best for you. All payment methods are secure and encrypted to protect your financial information.
Can I update my payment method after setting up AutoPay?
Yes. Log into your Progressive account, go to "Payment Methods" or "AutoPay Settings", and update your card or bank account information anytime. Changes take effect on your next scheduled payment date. You can also call 1(888)620-0950 to update payment methods over the phone.
